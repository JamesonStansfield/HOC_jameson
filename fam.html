John Atanasoff worked alongside a graduate student, Clifford Berry. He was born April 19th, 1918 and died October 30th, 1963 at just 45 years old. He was another American who also went to Iowa state college. Clifford Berry obtained his bachelors in electrical engineering and his masters in physics.

John Atanasoffs fathers name was Ivan Atanasoff. He was born in Boiajik, Bulgaria. When Ivan was 1 years old, his parents were killed in the Bulgarian War. When Ivan was around 13 years old, he immigrated to the United States of America and moved in with his uncle. While he was in the United States, he met his soon to be wife, named Iva Lucena Purdy. They had nine children together and one of which was John.

John Atanasoff grew up with his ggrowing interest and passion for engineering and math. At a young age, he would read college math books with his mother and learn concepts as he had a great interest in math. He carried on to enter the Mulberry High School and complete the entire school course in just 2 years with straight A's. He knew that he was going to study theoretic physics. As he grew up and went on to the University of Florida, he received his bachelors of science in electrical engineering. After this, many famous universities like Harvard reached out to him although he decided to go on a different path by taking a program more tutored towards his specific interests at Iowa state College. He was a very busy man during this time as he was taking his masters course and teaching two undergraduate programs at the same time. John was very successful in his education and work.
